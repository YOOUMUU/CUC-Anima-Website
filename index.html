Our students' work won the first prize in the 10th National Student Digital Media Technology and Creativity Competition

2023-02-21

In the 10th National Student Digital Media Technology and Creativity Competition National Finals, the work named DOLL HOUSE developed by our students Wang Yuqi, Wang Minghui, Wang Shi and Fu Jiaxin under the guidance of Professor Chen Jingwei, won the First Prize Award. The award ceremony of finals was held on November 26, 2022.

Two Animation Works from the Class of 2018 Nominated for the 2023 Stuttgart International Festival of Animated Film (ITFS)

2023-02-28

Recently, the official website of the Stuttgart International Festival of Animated Film (ITFS) released a list of nominees, in which two works from the Class of 2018 majoring in Animation at Communication University of China (CUC) are nominated for the 2023 Stuttgart International Festival of Animated Film (ITFS) in Germany.

The work from the School of Animation and Digital Arts has won the title of the best experimental short film in Beijing Inernational Film Festival • the 29th Beijing College Student Film Festival

2023-03-02

The winners of Beijing Inernational Film Festival • the 29th Beijing College Student Film Festival has announced by the official recently. The Film Zero: The Cyber Spirits instructed by Cui Yunpeng, directed by Zheng Yugao and Xiao Zhuxuan stood out from more than 2000 entries, and won the title of the best experimental short film in the 22nd College students original film selection.
