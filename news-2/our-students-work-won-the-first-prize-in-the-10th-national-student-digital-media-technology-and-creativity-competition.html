Our students' work won the first prize in the 10th National Student Digital Media Technology and Creativity Competition

2023-02-21

DOLL HOUSE is a 2D puzzle and narrative game with a Gothic comic style. The game tells a story of that a girl, Rebecca, is often immersed herself in the dollhouse in her bedroom, where she created her own small world. Princess Elle is the doll identified by Rebecca. Player needs to help Elle escape from the castle full of unknown and challenges.

‍

There are metaphors and connotations hidden everywhere in the game. In this small dollhouse, the puzzle is closely linked with the narrative. The gameplay and stories inside and outside the dollhouse also have an interpenetrating effect on each other. Players can analyze their own spiritual world inspired by this small family microcosm in the game, which is to think clearly what they want to escape from is, and what binds them. The author team expresses a main idea of breaking the traditional concept and escaping from bondage through this creative design of the game.
