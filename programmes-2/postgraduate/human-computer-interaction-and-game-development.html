Human-Computer Interaction and Game Development

Overview and Objectives of the Program

Overview

With the development philosophy of "humanities as body, science and technology as application, design as method", the major has formed an interdisciplinary ecological system with diversified symbiosis, and has formed the disciplinary characteristics and advantages of close integration between industry, academia, research and application, and has obtained good academic and social effects. This programme focuses on the frontiers of modern digital art creation and technology research and development, aiming to cultivate leading talents for China in the metaverse era, capable of carrying out independent research and creation in the frontiers of digital art and the combination of technology and art.

‍

Training Objective

This program course is dedicated to cultivating high-level professionals with solid theoretical foundation and adapting to the practical work needs of digital art-related industries or professions, such as interactive design and game development. It requires students to be familiar with the creation process and cutting-edge technology of digital art works, have high computer algorithm ability and certain humanistic art cultivation, and possess good computer programming, interactive art creation, design and communication, teamwork and management skills. Students in this course have the ability of computer programming, interactive art creation, design and communication, teamwork and management, etc. Graduates of this major can be employed in enterprises and institutions related to games, mobile applications, interactive advertising, virtual reality, interactive display and other industries.

  • Good moral character, academic integrity, general quality and innovative spirit.
  • A systematic understanding of the theoretical foundations of the discipline, a comprehensive knowledge of the discipline and insight into the frontiers of development in the discipline.
  • A mastery of a foreign language, the proficiency to read the literature of the profession, the competence in foreign language writing and international academic communication.
  • Students will have the ability to independently analyse and solve problems related to the theory and practice of their discipline, and will be able to perform independently research, creative work, design and research of a high standard.

‍

Areas of Research Focus

The research focuses on exploring the creation laws, creation methods and their technical principles of digital art works such as human-computer interaction and game development. It is required to carry out research on technical problems in the creation of various types of digital art works on the basis of theory and practice(such as video games, virtual reality, augmented reality, interactive devices, etc.), practice various digital art creation concepts and creation styles, and pay close attention to the impact of the development of interactive technology and artificial intelligence on digital art creation.

The research in this direction is characterized by close cooperation with the development of animation, games, film and television, tracking the latest advances in human-computer interaction technology, computer graphics, computer vision, and artificial intelligence technology, and achieving innovation in digital art concepts and technology from multiple perspectives.

The training contents of the graduate students in this direction include: mastering the realization method of developing digital art works by computer; systematically learning the creative thinking and planning ability necessary for digital art creation.

‍

Faculty

The program has an interdisciplinary faculty with high professional level, reasonable academic background, and a balance of art, technology and humanities. Most of the faculty members are engaged in game design, human-computer interaction, computer graphics, game development technology, design thinking, virtual reality, user experience, etc. Among them, there are 4 professors and 9 associate professors.

Research Platform

The program has the National Animation and Digital Media Practice Teaching Center, Beijing Key Laboratory of Digital Animation Technology Research, Unity Joint Laboratory, Beijing Radio New Media Research and Development Center, Integrated Media Demonstration Center, China Animation Art Exhibition Hall, China (Beijing) International Student Animation Festival and other institutions and programs. At present, it is also the secretariat unit of the Advanced Training Base of Digital Creativity of the Ministry of Culture of China, and the secretariat unit of the Teaching Steering Committee of Animation and Digital Media in Higher Education, the Education Committee of China Animation Society, the National Association of Computer Basic Education in Universities, the Animation and Digital Media Art Committee of the National University Film and Television Society, and the Digital Photography and Video Committee of the China Film and Television Technology Society. It can provide rich resources and diversified platforms for the study and research of postgraduates. In 2012, the professional construction unit was awarded the Best Animation Education Institution Award by the Chinese Culture and Arts Government.
