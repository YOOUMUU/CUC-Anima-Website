Programmes in CUC anima

Through continuous development, the School of Animation and Digital Arts has a complete system for training undergraduate, master and doctoral talents in animation and digital media art. Undergraduate education now has three professional groups: animation and comics, future media, and gaming and e-sports, with animation and comics under the animation and comics professional group, digital media art and new media art under the future media professional group, and digital media technology, game art, art and technology under the gaming and e-sports professional group. Game Development, Art and Science, Radio and Television and many other directions; PhD provides digital art, art and science and other professional directions.
